Contact DTF Genetics

Use verified DTF channels.

Official contact links, community invitations, product announcements, contests, and payment instructions are published only through DTF-controlled websites and verified DTF Genetics profiles.

What to include in a message

  • Order or product question: product name, order reference, and the website used. Never post payment details publicly.
  • Genetics question: line name, generation or package information, and the specific trait or documentation question.
  • Education correction: page title, exact statement, supporting source, and the proposed correction.
  • Game report: game name, device/browser, steps that caused the problem, and a screenshot when possible.
  • Community matter: event or channel name, date, and a concise description.
  • Business inquiry: organization, purpose, requested deliverable, and intended timeline.

Protect yourself

  • Do not send passwords, private keys, payment-card numbers, or account recovery codes.
  • Do not trust unsolicited direct messages offering private inventory, prizes, investments, or expedited access.
  • Confirm that checkout and login pages remain on a DTF-controlled domain.
  • Report impersonation through the platform where it appears.
