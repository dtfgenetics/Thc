DTF Genetics

Genetics built through selection, observation, and documentation.

This catalog introduces active DTF Genetics breeding projects and the traits being studied in each line. Availability, generation, and seed type must be confirmed on the individual product page before any purchase.

Blue Mango

Lineage: Somango XXL × Blueberry Butcher

Breeding direction: vigorous plants, strong branching, heavy resin production, substantial flower structure, and a candy-blueberry-meets-ripe-mango aromatic profile.

Observed flowering window: commonly described within an 8–10 week range under suitable conditions. Phenotype and environment can change actual finish time.

Blue Mango is a flagship DTF Genetics project developed through multiple filial generations. Generation, regular/feminized status, inventory, and release details will be stated on the specific listing when available.

Mango Bubbles

Lineage: Blue Mango × Blue Bubblegum

Breeding direction: combine the fruit-forward mango and blueberry character of Blue Mango with the sweet bubblegum influence of Blue Bubblegum.

Mango Bubbles is an active DTF Genetics line. Formal release details will identify the exact generation and seed type.

Blue Bubblegum

Lineage: Bubblegum Kush × Blueberry Butcher

Blue Bubblegum contributes a sweet, fruit-and-bubblegum direction to the DTF breeding catalog and serves as a parent in Mango Bubbles.

Blueberry Butcher

Lineage: Blueberry Muffin × Jack Herer

Blueberry Butcher is an important parent in the DTF catalog and contributes to both Blue Mango and Blue Bubblegum.

How to read this catalog

  • Lineage identifies the documented parents used in the DTF breeding project.
  • Breeding direction describes selection goals and repeated observations, not a promise that every seed will express the same traits.
  • Generation and seed type can differ between releases and must be checked on the exact listing.
  • Flowering estimates are planning ranges, not fixed harvest dates.
  • Grow reports should be treated as observations from specific plants and environments.
