Image standard

  • Use original, licensed, public-domain, or properly authorized images.
  • Identify AI-generated or illustrated concepts when they could be mistaken for grow documentation.
  • Do not present a stock photo as a DTF plant, cultivar, facility, or product.
  • Preserve creator credit and submission permission.
  • Use descriptive alternative text so the gallery remains accessible.
