DTF Community

Grow together. Learn together. Build together.

The DTF community connects growers, breeders, players, artists, and curious adults through cultivation education, grow documentation, friendly competitions, game testing, and creative participation.

Community activities

Grow-offs

Structured community events give members a reason to document their plants, compare methods, share observations, and celebrate improvement. Every event uses published rules, submission requirements, and judging criteria.

Teaching Healthy Cultivation

Community discussion supports the education library by identifying common questions, documenting real-world observations, and separating repeatable principles from unsupported claims.

Game nights and playtesting

Players help test DTF browser and tabletop games, report confusing rules or technical problems, and vote on future improvements.

Creative participation

Community members may contribute grow photos, questions, design feedback, challenge entries, and educational topic requests when a submission window is open.

Community standard

  • Adults only.
  • Follow local laws and platform rules.
  • Share observations honestly and label uncertainty.
  • Do not pressure members into purchases, trades, or private transactions.
  • Do not present medical, pesticide, electrical, or legal claims as professional advice.
  • Critique ideas and methods without attacking people.
  • Respect creators, breeders, photographers, and artists by crediting original work.

Use verified DTF channels

Official invitations, contests, prizes, releases, and payment instructions will only be announced through verified DTF Genetics channels. Do not trust unsolicited direct messages claiming to represent DTF Genetics.
